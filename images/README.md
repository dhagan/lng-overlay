# Images Needed for Full Visual Impact

To complete the visual comparison mockups, add these images to this folder:

## Required Images:

### 1. `beach-pristine.jpg`
**What to search for:** "Hawaii beach pristine ocean view sunset clear horizon"
- Use: Shows what we have NOW - beautiful, unobstructed ocean views
- Suggested sources:
  - Unsplash.com (free, high quality)
  - Pexels.com (free stock photos)
  - Search: "waikiki beach sunset" or "hawaii coastline pristine"

### 2. `lng-industrial.jpg`
**What to search for:** "LNG FSRU ship offshore industrial facility"
- Use: Shows what it will look like AFTER - massive industrial structure
- Suggested sources:
  - Search "LNG FSRU" on Google Images
  - Look for photos showing the massive scale
  - Good example: FSRU vessels are 900+ feet long
  - Alternative search: "offshore LNG terminal" or "floating LNG facility"

### 3. `beach-paradise.jpg`
**What to search for:** "Hawaii beach tourists swimming paradise"
- Use: Shows current beach experience - families, recreation
- Sources: Unsplash/Pexels
- Search: "hawaii beach activities" or "waikiki beach swimmers"

### 4. `industrial-coast.jpg`
**What to search for:** "industrial coastal facility power plant smoke"
- Use: Shows what onshore facilities look like - ugly, polluting
- Sources: Google Images
- Search: "coastal power plant" or "industrial waterfront facility"

## Free Stock Photo Sites:
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/) - No attribution required
- [Pexels](https://pexels.com/) - Free for any use
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/) - Free images

## Tips:
1. Download high-resolution versions (at least 1920px wide)
2. Rename them exactly as listed above
3. Place them in this `/images` folder
4. The comparison will automatically show the images with text overlay

## Current State:
Without images, the mockups show text descriptions. With images, they'll create a powerful visual impact showing the dramatic before/after difference.
